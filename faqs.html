What does Globetrotter do exactly?

Well, you tell Globetrotter where you want to travel, for how long, what you want to see (or what kinds of things you want to see) and Globetrotter coordinates pretty much everything, flights, events, hotels, you name it.








What locations does Globetrotter recommend?

All of the locations offered by Globetrotter are selected for their satisfaction. If you want some advice in picking the right location for you, check out our How To page.



How often does your newsletter go out?

If you are on Globetrotter's mailing list, then you should receive an email from Globetrotter twice a month featuring deals and new destinations.



What are the benefits of Globetrotter?

Globetrotter does all the hard work and can even get better deals that some might miss on hotel rooms, attraction tours, flights, and more. The best part is that you get granular control over what you want Globetrotter to organize and what you think might be best in your own hands.
